## Description of the Akan-Name-Generator
**AKAN-NAMING SYSTEM** come from <ins>Ghanaian culture</ins>, where children are named based on the day they were born and their gender.The Akan name tradition comes from the Akan people, one of the largest ethnic groups in West Africa, mainly in Ghana and parts of Côte d’Ivoire. Its origins are deeply rooted in history, spirituality, and social identity.
