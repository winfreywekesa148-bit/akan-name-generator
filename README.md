## Description of the Akan-Name-Generator
**AKAN-NAMING SYSTEM** come from <ins>Ghanaian culture</ins>, where children are named based on the day they were born and their gender.The Akan name tradition comes from <ins>the Akan people, one of the largest ethnic groups in West Africa</ins>, mainly in Ghana and parts of Côte d’Ivoire. Its origins are deeply rooted in history, spirituality, and social identity.
### Instructions
1. Read the homepage to understand the background and ethnicity of the Akan-names.
2. If interested, fill the form with correct, accurate and valid data to know your Akan-Name.
3. The name will be displayed at the bottom of the logo.
